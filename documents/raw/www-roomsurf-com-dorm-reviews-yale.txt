“
Stiles is an underrated college at Yale. The people there really make it a wonderful place to live. The rooms are spacious and the common areas are a great place to hang out with friends. The staff is so welcoming, and this year, were willing to go out of their way to make the most for students. This included running weekly testing, providing to-go meal options, all while still make Stiles the best residential college at Yale.
